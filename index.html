COVID-19 Visualization

COVID-19 is the disease caused by a new coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. WHO first learned of this new virus on 31 December 2019, following a report of a cluster of cases of ‘viral pneumonia’ in Wuhan, People’s Republic of China. While many countries managed to bring the first wave of this disease under control, until the vaccine safely reaches everyone, we have far from won this battle.
